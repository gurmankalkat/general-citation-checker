The technology industry has shaped the global economy over the past several decades.

Apple was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in Cupertino, California. The company went public in December 1980, and its IPO was one of the largest in Silicon Valley history at the time. In 2023, Apple became the first publicly traded company to reach a market capitalization of $3 trillion.

Google handles over 90 percent of global internet search queries as of 2024. The company was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were PhD students at Stanford University. Alphabet, Google's parent company, reported annual revenue of $250 billion in 2023.

The electric vehicle market has grown significantly in recent years. Tesla delivered 1.2 million vehicles in 2023, making it one of the leading electric vehicle manufacturers globally.

Many people find working in technology rewarding. I personally think the pace of innovation is remarkable.
